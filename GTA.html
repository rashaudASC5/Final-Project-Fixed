Overview

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an open-world action-adventure video game that takes place in a fictional city called Los Santos. Players take control of three protagonists, Michael, Franklin, and Trevor, and can switch between them to complete various missions and heists. The game also features a vast world to explore, a robust online multiplayer mode, and a variety of side activities like sports, minigames, and more. The Online counterpart is very similar to GTA 5 but with personalized characters and the ability to play with friends!!!
